About Us

Triloka Arts and Sports Club, Vellimon, is a vibrant and dynamic club that came into existence in 2021. With a strong focus on promoting both artistic and athletic pursuits, Triloka offers a diverse range of activities and opportunities for individuals of all ages and backgrounds. The club serves as a haven for artists, athletes, and enthusiasts alike, fostering creativity, physical fitness, and a sense of community. Through its extensive roster of programs, including visual arts, performing arts, and various sports disciplines, Triloka aims to inspire and nurture talent, while also providing a platform for members to showcase their skills and passion. Since its inception, Triloka has become a hub for cultural exchange, personal growth, and the celebration of artistic expression and sporting excellence. With its unwavering commitment to providing a holistic and inclusive experience, Triloka Arts and Sports Club continues to thrive as a beloved institution, uniting individuals through their shared love for arts and sports in the vibrant community of Vellimon.
